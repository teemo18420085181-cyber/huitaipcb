Why does MPN matter in PCBA BOM sourcing?

MPN means manufacturer part number. It helps identify the exact component, not just a general part description.

Without clear MPNs, suppliers may quote different brands, packages, tolerances, or electrical specifications.

Clear BOM data helps reduce sourcing risk, replacement confusion, and quotation delays.

A useful BOM should include MPN, quantity, package, reference designator, and acceptable alternatives.

For turnkey PCBA, send Gerber, BOM, quantity, and testing requirements for review.
