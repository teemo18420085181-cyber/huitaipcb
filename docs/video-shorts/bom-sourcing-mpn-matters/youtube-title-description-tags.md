## Recommended Title

Why MPN Matters in PCBA BOM Sourcing

## Description

MPN means manufacturer part number. In PCBA BOM sourcing, a clear MPN helps identify the exact component, not just a general part description.

Without clear MPNs, suppliers may quote different brands, packages, tolerances, or electrical specifications. A useful BOM should include MPN, quantity, package, reference designator, and acceptable alternatives.
