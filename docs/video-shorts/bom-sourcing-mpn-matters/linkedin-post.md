Clear MPNs can make a PCBA BOM much easier to quote and review.

MPN means manufacturer part number. It helps identify the exact component instead of relying on a broad description such as package size, value, or function alone.

For PCBA BOM sourcing, unclear MPNs may lead to different quoted brands, packages, tolerances, or electrical specifications. A more useful BOM usually includes:

- MPN
- Quantity
- Package
- Reference designator
- Acceptable alternatives
- Notes for unavailable or flexible parts

For turnkey PCBA manufacturing, it also helps to send Gerber files, BOM, quantity, and testing requirements together for review.
