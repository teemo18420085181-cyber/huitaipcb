# Video Prompts

## Full Video Prompt

Create a 9:16 vertical animated PCBA knowledge explainer video, 35-38 seconds, English voice-over and English subtitles. Topic: "BOM Sourcing for PCBA: Why MPN Matters." Use a friendly HT PCBA Bot character if available. Visual style should be blue, white, and gray industrial technology with small yellow and green Huitai Electronics accent arcs. Use animated BOM tables, component labels, datasheet cards, PCB traces, and clean checklist graphics. Do not use or imply real factory footage.

Narrative:

1. 0-3s: Ask, "Why does MPN matter in PCBA BOM sourcing?"
2. 3-8s: Explain that MPN means manufacturer part number and identifies the exact component.
3. 8-14s: Show that unclear MPNs can cause different brands, packages, or specifications to be quoted.
4. 14-20s: Show clean BOM data reducing sourcing risk, replacement confusion, and quotation delays.
5. 20-28s: Show a useful BOM with MPN, quantity, package, reference designator, and acceptable alternatives.
6. 28-35s: For turnkey PCBA, send Gerber, BOM, quantity, and testing requirements for review.
7. 35-38s: End card with huitaipcb.com/bom-sourcing-pcb-assembly.

Avoid words such as cheapest, fastest, best, guaranteed, number one, top manufacturer, perfect quality, no risk, and 100% defect-free.

## Scene-by-scene Prompt

- Scene 1: HT PCBA Bot points at a BOM spreadsheet and asks why MPN matters.
- Scene 2: A manufacturer part number label connects an exact component to its datasheet.
- Scene 3: A vague BOM row branches into multiple possible parts to show quotation mismatch risk.
- Scene 4: A clean BOM row removes warning icons and reduces quotation delay.
- Scene 5: Checklist cards appear: MPN, quantity, package, reference designator, acceptable alternatives.
- Scene 6: Gerber, BOM, quantity, and testing icons flow into a review checklist.
- Scene 7: Huitai Electronics end card with service page URL.
