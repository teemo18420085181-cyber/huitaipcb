## CTA Patterns

Common CTA types:

- Online quote calculator
- Upload Gerber/BOM
- Get an instant quote
- Contact sales
- Request quote
- Explore capabilities

Huitai should not pretend to offer instant automated pricing if the real process is engineering review before quotation. The better CTA is:

- Upload Gerber & BOM for Engineering Review
- Send Your BOM for Sourcing Risk Review
- Request a Low-Volume PCBA Review
- Upload Prototype Files for Review

## Huitai Strategic Gap

Huitai should avoid a pure broad-keyword fight against PCBWay/JLCPCB/NextPCB. The near-term opportunity is the intersection of:

- Existing Huitai page basis.
- Real GSC impressions or clicks.
- Public market/buyer question signal.
- High business fit.
- Conversion path to Gerber + BOM upload or WhatsApp/email.

Best immediate cluster:

1. BOM sourcing and shortage review.
2. Quote/file-preparation intent.
3. Prototype and low-volume PCBA.
4. China turnkey PCBA supplier positioning.

Near-term page gap:

- `/china-pcb-assembly` deserves follow-up because it has 97 GSC impressions and zero clicks.
- File-preparation and quote checklist articles rank or show impressions but need stronger CTA routes.
