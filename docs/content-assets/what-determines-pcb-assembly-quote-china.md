## Quick Answer

A PCB assembly quote in China is shaped by the PCB specifications, BOM cost and sourcing difficulty, SMT setup, component package density, testing requirements, order quantity, lead time, and the engineering review time needed to confirm the project. Two boards of the same size can differ several times in price once part count, fine-pitch components, and test scope are considered. That is why an accurate quote depends on complete files rather than an instant online estimate.

## Main factors that affect a PCB assembly quote

- **PCB specifications** — material, finish, and tolerances
- **PCB layer count** and **board size**
- **BOM cost** — usually the largest single factor
- **Component sourcing difficulty** — obsolete, long-lead, or single-source parts
- **SMT setup cost** — stencil and line setup
- **Component package density** — fine-pitch, BGA, double-sided
- **Through-hole (DIP) assembly** requirements
- **Testing requirements** — visual, AOI, functional
- **Quantity** — prototype, low volume, or batch
- **Lead time** — expedited builds cost more
- **Packaging** and labeling
- **Engineering review time** to confirm scope

## Quotation checklist

Before requesting a quote, check:

- Gerber files ready?
- BOM complete with manufacturer part numbers?
- Quantity confirmed?
- Alternative components allowed?
- Testing method available?
- Target delivery time confirmed?
- Sample photos available if files are incomplete?

## Common quote delays

- Incomplete BOM
- Unclear components
- Unavailable or obsolete ICs
- No test instruction
- Missing quantity
- Unclear assembly side (top/bottom)

The clearer your files, the more accurate your PCB assembly quote. Send Gerber files, a complete BOM, quantity, lead time, and testing requirements together. If files are incomplete, Huitai Electronics can review a partial BOM or sample photos first.
